ECKANKAR in New Jersey

Workshops

All workshops are scheduled periodically and are also available on request. You can read about the purpose and interactive format of all our workshops on both our activities and presentations pages. Currently available workshop topics include the following.

Have You Had a Spiritual Experience?

Out-of-body or near-death experience? Dreams of a departed loved one? A sense you’ve lived before? An inner blue light or inner sound?

  • Discover what their experiences really mean
  • Connect with other like-minded Souls
  • Learn new and advanced ways for exploring their inner worlds

How to Survive Spiritually in Our Times

Spiritual tools for challenging times. Participants will explore techniques for:

  • Opening the heart for greater guidance
  • Learning how to listen to the Voice of God
  • Not only surviving, but thriving spiritually

Past Lives, Dreams and Soul Travel

Participants will experience spiritual exercises to learn:

  • How past lives affect them today
  • How dreams can solve daily problems
  • The secrets of Soul Travel to discover who they are as Soul

Spiritual Wisdom on Health and Healing

Participants will:

  • Explore the purpose of spiritual healing
  • Learn how to arrange conditions that are best for spiritual growth
  • Learn a spiritual exercise to help them see themselves and their state of health from a higher perspective
  • Share their stories and experiences

The Art of Spiritual Dreaming

Dreams can help every aspect of your life. Participants will learn how to:

  • Understand the hidden guidance in dreams
  • Remember and interpret dreams
  • Use dreams to see and change their future (based on the book, The Art of Spiritual Dreaming, by Harold Klemp)
